About the Awards
2nd Annual · 2026
The hardest work in the world deserves the loudest recognition.

Cause Catalyst Awards are presented annually to human services organizations across North America — recognizing nonprofits that lead with courage, listen to the people they serve, and prove that impact is built, not accidental.

Winners are scored by independent sector leaders on measurable outcomes, client-centered practices, and organizational commitment to continuous improvement. Nominations are open to any qualifying nonprofit or human services program in the US or Canada.
