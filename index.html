"The organizations doing the hardest work are often the most invisible. These awards exist to change that."

Cause Catalyst Awards are presented annually to organizations that demonstrate an unwavering commitment to their clients — not just in outcomes, but in how they listen, adapt, and lead.

Winners are selected by a panel of sector leaders and evaluated on measurable impact, client-centered practices, and commitment to continuous improvement.

Nominations are open to any nonprofit or human services program operating in North America.

Award Categories

Five categories recognizing different dimensions of impact in human services.

Organization of the Year

Holistic commitment to client outcomes, staff culture, and community trust.

Innovation in Impact

A bold, data-informed program model that measurably shifted outcomes for those served.

Excellence in Listening

Genuine, ongoing feedback loops with clients — and evidence of acting on what was heard.

Rising Impact Award

Organizations under five years old already producing outsized, measurable impact.

Leadership Excellence

An individual leader whose vision has transformed their organization and the broader sector.
