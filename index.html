About the Awards

Recognition for the real work.

Cause Catalyst Awards are presented annually to human services organizations that demonstrate an unwavering commitment to their clients — not just in outcomes, but in how they listen, adapt, and lead.

Winners are selected by a panel of sector leaders and evaluated on measurable impact, client-centered practices, and their commitment to continuous improvement.

Nominations are open to any nonprofit or human services program operating in North America.

Award Categories

Five ways to stand out.

01 🌟
Organization of

Awarded to the organization demonstrating the most holistic commitment to client outcomes, staff culture, and community trust.

02 💡
Innovation in
Impact

Recognizing a bold, data-informed program model or service delivery approach that meaningfully shifted outcomes for those served.

03 🎤
Excellence in
Listening

Honoring organizations that have built genuine, ongoing feedback loops with clients — and acted on what they heard.

04 🌱
Rising
Impact Award

For organizations less than five years old that have already demonstrated outsized impact relative to their size and resources.

05 🤝
Leadership

Recognizing an individual leader whose vision, humility, and courage have transformed their organization and inspired the broader sector.
